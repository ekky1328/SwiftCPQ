Roadmap Features

My primary focus is to deliver an MVP first. Once that's done, I'll start prioritizing additional features based on what's most beneficial.

MVP Release

Intuitive Editor

Create stunning, professional quotes effortlessly with an editor that's as simple as it is powerful. Built with a battle-tested web components, our editor ensures you can craft polished proposals quickly, with no learning curve. Impress clients with every quote, all while saving time.

MVP Release

Version History

Never worry about losing track of changes again. We'll let you easily roll back to any previous version of your quote. Whether you're refining a proposal or need to undo a mistake, accessing a version from earlier is quick and simple.

MVP Release

Product Catalogue

Build and maintain a dynamic catalogue of products and services that grows with your business, allowing you to quickly pull from a library of pre-defined items for consistent and accurate quoting. Making it easier to create tailored proposals, and ensures consistency across all sales reps.

MVP Release

Flexible Recurrence

Offer your clients a range of pricing options; upfront, monthly, or annual, to suit their preferences and maximize flexibility. Our recurrence options ensure you can tailor your pricing strategy to match both your business needs and your clients’ expectations.

MVP Release

Pricing Tools

Whether you're needing to apply percentage-based or fixed-price margins, or offering customized discounts, We allow you to tailor pricing for every scenario. Price with confidence and maximize profitability without the guesswork.

MVP Release

Stunning Proposals

Generate stunning PDF proposals with ease, choosing from our designed standard templates or creating fully customized ones that reflect your brand identity. Whether you need a quick quote or a fully branded proposal, our flexible templates have you covered.

Roadmapped

Quote Approval Workflow

An approval process for quotes, ensuring that sales managers or technical staff can review and approve proposals before they are sent to clients for enhanced accuracy and consistency.

Roadmapped

Signature Integration

Integrate electronic signature functionality to streamline the approval process. Clients can sign directly on the proposal, making it faster to finalize deals and reducing paperwork.

Roadmapped

Web Proposal View

Share proposals online with clients through a sleek and professional web view for better engagement.

Roadmapped

Agreement Management

Easily adjust and customize agreements to match client needs, then convert those changes into quotes or proposals ready for client approval. Perfect for MSPs who need flexibility in service agreements and quick adaptation to client requirements.

Roadmapped

Contract Generation

Once a quote is finalized, automatically generate a contract with pre-populated data, reducing manual work and ensuring your documents are ready to go when you need them.

Roadmapped

Custom Fields

Add custom fields to quotes, allowing you to capture unique information for each proposal. This ensures flexibility and helps you tailor quotes to meet specific client needs or business requirements.

Roadmapped

REST API

Whether you need to sync data, automate processes, or connect with third-party software, our API will provide the flexibility to extend functionality, giving you full control over your sales workflow.

Roadmapped

Data Export Options

Easily export your proposals and data in multiple formats, including CSV, JSON, and more. Simplify reporting, integrate with other tools, or archive your data effortlessly to suit your workflow.

Roadmapped

Client Portal

Offer clients access to their proposals and quotes through a self-service portal where they can review, approve, or request changes to their documents at any time.

Roadmapped

Product Bundling

Create product bundles with custom discounts for upselling and cross-selling opportunities, enabling you to offer complete solutions to your clients.

Roadmapped

Analytics Dashboard

Track key metrics like quote-to-close ratio, sales funnels, forecasting, average deal size, and proposal status to measure and improve your sales performance.

Roadmapped

Bulk Quote Generation

Generate multiple quotes at once for large customer bases, saving time and effort. Perfect for sending tailored quotes to several clients at once while maintaining accuracy and consistency.

Roadmapped

Milestone Payments

Generate quotes with milestone payments, allowing you to break down large projects into manageable payments for clients. Perfect for projects that require multiple stages or deliverables. It makes for an easier sell and ensures you get paid on time.

Roadmapped

AI/LLM Integration

Integrate AI-powered tools to analyze your quotes and proposals, providing recommendations to help you quote faster and improve your sales process. We'll keep it as an optional feature, so you can choose to use it or not, we know AI isn't for everyone.

Roadmapped

Inventory

Manage your inventory and stock levels directly within SwiftCPQ, ensuring you can accurately quote products and services based on real-time availability. Perfect for businesses that need to manage stock levels and ensure accurate pricing.

Committed to Open Source & Independent Development

This project will stay open-source, independent, and focused on the needs of ICT consultants and growing MSPs.

It will always be community-oriented, open, and supportive. My goal is to help SMBs keep money in their pockets, instead of being exploited year after year by price hikes, shady business practices, or contracts that result from tools being swallowed up by giant corporations like Kaseya, Datto, Connectwise, and ScalePad.

Don't get me wrong, there is nothing wrong with getting paid and making money, but rarely does an acquisition benefit the end-user and that is ultimately what I wish to avoid.
